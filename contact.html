Frequently Asked

I purchased something but did not receive it.

Open the app, go to Shop, and tap "Restore Purchases." If the issue persists, contact support@voidgarden.com with your Apple ID.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to submit a request.

I discovered a bug. Where should I report it?

Email support@voidgarden.com with your device model, iOS version, and a description of what occurred.

When is your next game releasing?

We are always building. Visit our games page for the current status of each title.

May I use your game footage for content creation?

Yes — we welcome it. You are free to create videos, streams, and content featuring our games without asking permission.
