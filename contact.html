// FAQ


I bought something but didn't receive it. What do I do?

Open the app, go to Shop, and tap "Restore Purchases." This restores all non-consumable purchases. If the issue persists, email support@voidgarden.com with your Apple ID and we'll sort it out.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund directly from Apple.

I found a bug. Where do I report it?

Email support@voidgarden.com with your device model, iOS version, and a description of the bug. Screenshots or screen recordings are very helpful.

When is your next game coming out?

We're always building. Check our games page for the latest status on each title. Follow us for announcements.

Can I use your game footage for content creation?

Yes — we actively encourage it. You're free to create videos, streams, and content featuring our games. No need to ask permission. We'd love to see what you make — tag us if you share.
