Information We Collect

  • Usage data — gameplay statistics, session length, scores, and in-app behaviour to improve performance and experience.
  • Device information — device type, OS version, and unique identifiers for analytics and crash reporting.
  • Purchase data — processed securely through Apple's App Store. We do not store payment information.
  • Game progress — scores, unlocked items, and preferences stored locally on your device.
  • Camera and photo data (ShroomScan only) — photos are processed by our AI service and not stored after processing.

Advertising

Our free apps display advertisements served by Google AdMob. You can opt out of personalised advertising in your device Settings under Privacy & Security → Tracking. All apps offer a Remove Ads purchase ($1.99) to permanently disable advertising.

Our apps offer optional purchases including coin packs, cosmetic skins, and ad removal. All purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not store payment information.

  • Non-consumable purchases (skins, remove ads) can be restored via Restore Purchases in-app
