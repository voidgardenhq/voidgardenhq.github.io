Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

VoidGarden ("we", "us", or "our") is an independent mobile game studio. This Privacy Policy covers all applications developed and published by VoidGarden, including:

⚡ Void Sprint — Endless Platformer
🌸 Sakura Drop — Helix Puzzle Game
🪐 Fling — Orbit & Drift Game
🍄 ShroomScan — AI Mushroom Identifier

Information We Collect

Our apps may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage data — gameplay statistics, session length, scores, and in-app behaviour to improve performance and user experience.
  • Device information — device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers used for analytics and crash reporting.
  • Purchase data — in-app purchase history processed securely through Apple's App Store. We do not store payment information.
  • Game progress — scores, unlocked items, and preferences stored locally on your device.
  • Camera and photo data (ShroomScan only) — photos are sent to our AI service for processing and are not stored after processing is complete.

Advertising

Our free apps display advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to show relevant ads. You can opt out of personalised advertising in your device Settings under Privacy & Security → Tracking.

All apps offer a Remove Ads purchase ($1.99) to permanently disable advertising.

In-App Purchases

Our apps offer optional purchases including coin packs, cosmetic items, and ad removal. All purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not store payment information.

  • Consumable purchases (coin packs) cannot be restored
  • Non-consumable purchases (skins, remove ads) can be restored via the Restore Purchases option in-app

Data Storage

Most data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers storing personal user data beyond purchase verification via RevenueCat. Deleting the app removes all local data.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Contact us at hello@voidgarden.com if you believe this has occurred.

If you are in the EEA, you have rights to access, rectify, erase, object to processing, and port your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights. Most data can be deleted by uninstalling the app.
