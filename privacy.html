Privacy Policy

What we collect, what we don't, and how to reach us.

VoidGarden builds atmospheric mobile games. This policy explains what data our games and website handle. It applies to every app published under the VoidGarden name and to voidgarden.com.

Plain language first, legal precision second. If anything here is unclear, write to hello@voidgarden.com and we will rewrite it.

The Short Version

  • VoidGarden games do not ask for your name, email, address, phone number, or any account login.
  • Games store gameplay data (high scores, coin balances, unlocked skins, settings) on your device only. We never see it.
  • Ads inside the games are served by Google AdMob, which uses standard ad identifiers.
  • In-app purchases are handled by Apple and (where used) by RevenueCat. Apple is the payment processor — VoidGarden never sees your card.
  • The website does not run analytics, tracking pixels, or cross-site cookies.

What the Games Collect

On-device only

Each game stores progress data in iOS UserDefaults on your device: high scores, coin balances, unlocked skins, sound and haptics preferences, ad-removal status. This data never leaves the device. Delete the app and it is gone.

Through Apple

When you make an in-app purchase, Apple handles the transaction through StoreKit. Apple shares with us only what is needed to verify the purchase: a receipt and product identifier. We do not receive your name, email, or payment details. See Apple's privacy policy for how Apple handles your account.

Through RevenueCat

Some games use RevenueCat to manage subscription and purchase state. RevenueCat receives an anonymized identifier and your StoreKit receipt. It does not receive personal information. RevenueCat's privacy policy is at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Through AdMob

Games show ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may use a device advertising identifier, approximate location derived from IP, and standard ad-serving data. You can reset or limit this identifier in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, and in Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising. Google's policy is at policies.google.com/privacy.

App Tracking Transparency

On first launch, each game requests the iOS ATT prompt. Decline and personalized ads turn off — you will still see ads, but contextual ones. Accept and ads may be personalized. Either choice is fully respected.

What the Website Collects

The site at voidgarden.com is hosted on GitHub Pages behind Cloudflare. It runs no analytics scripts, no marketing pixels, no third-party trackers. Standard server logs (IP address, request, timestamp) are kept by GitHub and Cloudflare per their policies — VoidGarden does not access or use them.

Children's Privacy

VoidGarden games are rated for general audiences. No game collects information knowingly from children. Games do not include sign-up forms, chat, or any personal-information entry field. If you believe a child has somehow been harmed by a VoidGarden game, contact us immediately at hello@voidgarden.com.

Your Rights

Because we don't hold personal data in any server we control, most data-rights requests don't apply. If you have purchased something through one of our games and want a record of the transaction, Apple is the seller of record and will help with refunds, receipts, and purchase history through the App Store. For anything we can clarify or correct, write to hello@voidgarden.com and we will respond.
